Scenario

You currently work at MOJ. You have applied for and been offered a job at HMRC. To progress your application, you have been asked to complete an online form.

You completed the first section of the form 2 days ago and you’ve since been notified via email you are now required to complete the next section.

The first section covered your personal details, and Security Clearance status.

Task

Can you show and tell me how you would fill out the form?

Think aloud. I’ll ask you some specific questions as you progress through the form.

Use your real information wherever possible; this is a dummy version of the service and nothing will be stored. This is to help us understand how accessible the data we are asking for is.

The screens that play the information back to you are pre-populated with dummy data.
