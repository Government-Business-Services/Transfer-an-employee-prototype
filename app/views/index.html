Scenario

You are in a HR team and a Line Manager has informed you that one of their staff who is currently on loan is moving to another government department.

The Line Manager has sent you data from them and the employee. You are now required to provide additional data to support the new department in onboarding the employee correctly.

Task

Can you show and tell me how you would fill out the form?
