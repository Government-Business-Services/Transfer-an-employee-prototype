This section is for HR departments transferring employees across the Civil Service.

In this section

You will review:
  • the employee’s answers to sections 1 and 2
  • their current line manager’s answers to Section 3

To complete Section 4 you also need some information about the employee.

You will need their:
  • start date in the Civil Service
  • loan end date (for employees already on loan to another department)
  • contract type
  • parental leave details (if any)
  • absence history
  • pay zone allowances and advances of salary and deductions, pay award (if any)
  • pension details

You can complete Section 4 all at once or return later.
