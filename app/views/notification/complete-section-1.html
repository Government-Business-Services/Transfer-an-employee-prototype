Complete the Civil Service staff transfer form

Congratulations on your new role. You now need to complete the staff transfer form. The form covers your HR, payroll and security clearance transfer.

There are four sections to the form. You complete Sections 1 and 2. Your line manager and HR/shared services complete Sections 3 and 4.

To complete Section 1 you'll need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • current department employee number
  • Baseline Personnel Security Standard (BPSS) status
  • level of security clearance
  • last 2 months of payslips
  • new department and job details

Guidance on the staff transfer process is on GOV.UK including:

  • what to expect
  • how to complete the form
  • a step by step guide to the process

    • To progress your transfer you should now complete Section 1 of the staff transfer form. You can complete this section all at once or return later.
