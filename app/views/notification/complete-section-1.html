Complete the Civil Service staff transfer form

Congratulations on your new role. You now need to complete Section 1 of the staff transfer form.

The form covers your HR, payroll and security clearance transfer. Your line manager and HR/shared services also have sections of the form to complete.

To complete Section 1 you'll need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • current department employee number
  • Baseline Personnel Security Standard (BPSS) status
  • level of security clearance
  • last 2 months of payslips
  • new department and job details

You can complete this section all at once or return later.

Guidance on the staff transfer process, including how to complete the form is on GOV.UK
