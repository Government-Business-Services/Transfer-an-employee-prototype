Completion of staff transfer form (Section 4) needed

Sarah Phillips has accepted a provisional job offer in another Government department. They have completed the first 2 sections of the Civil Service staff transfer form. Their line manager has completed Section 3.

What happens next

You now need to complete Section 4 of the staff transfer form

In this section

You need to review:

  • the employee’s answers to Sections 1 and 2
  • their current manager’s answers to Section 3

To complete Section 4, you will also need some information about the employee. You will need the employee’s:

  • start date in the Civil Service
  • loan end date (for employees already on loan to another department)
  • contract type
  • parental leave details (if any)
  • absence history
  • pay zone allowances and advances of salary and deductions, pay award (if any)
  • pension details

You can complete this section all at once or rerturn later

Timescale

Once you have completed Section 4, send the whole form to the Shared Services / HR team in the employee's new department. You should do this within 5 days.

Guidance on the staff transfer process, including how to complete the form is on GOV.UK
