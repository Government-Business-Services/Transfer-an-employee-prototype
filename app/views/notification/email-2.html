Staff transfer: Section 1 completed

You have now completed the Section 1 of the staff transfer form.

What happens next

Your transfer form has been sent to the vacancy holder in your new department.

Your new department will:

  • progress your pre-employment check
  • contact you to agree a start date and salary

Your start date should be the first day of the month where possible.

When your start date and salary are confirmed by email, you will need to complete Section 2 of the staff transfer form. You will receive an email telling you when you need to do this.

Guidance on the staff transfer process is on GOV.UK including:

  • what to expect
  • how to complete the form
  • a step by step guide to the process
