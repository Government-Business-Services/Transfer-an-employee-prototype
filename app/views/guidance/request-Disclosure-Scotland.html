How to move roles across the Civil Service: request Disclosure Scotland - email template

This page for Goverment Recruitment Services provices the email template for a Disclosure Scotland request.

Who does this email apply to?

It applies to civil servants who are transferring permanently or on a loan of more than 6 months, and need a Disclosure Scotland check as part of their transfer to another government department.

Use the wording provided for emails to transferring employees who need a Disclosure Scotland check as part of the staff transfer process.

Requesting Disclosure Scotland - email wording

Dear [path]application.user.formdata.1:1:1.value[/path],
Re: [path]application.opportunity.id[/path] [path]application.opportunity.title_labels.label_text[/path]


As part of your pre-employment checks you need to download the Disclosure Scotland application form, and complete sections 1 to 5:

https://www.mygov.scot/email-disclosure-application-form

For ‘application type’ select [‘standard DS’/'enhanced' - delete as appropriate] - this is the level of check undertaken by your recruiting department.

When you have completed sections 1 to 5 of the form, email it to pre-employmentchecks.grs@cabinetoffice.gov.uk with ‘Disclosure Scotland’ and your full name in the subject field.

If you have any questions about this please do not hesitate to contact us at the above email address.


Kind regards

Government Recruitment Service
