How to move roles across the Civil Service: request Disclosure Scotland - email template

This page for Goverment Recruitment Services provices the email template for a Disclosure Scotland request for ESS only.

Who does this email apply to?

It applies to civil servants who are transferring permanently or on a loan of more than 6 months, and need a Disclosure Scotland check as part of their transfer to another government department.

Use the wording provided for emails to transferring employees who need a Disclosure Scotland check as part of the staff transfer process.

Requesting Disclosure Scotland: ESS only - email wording

Dear [path]application.user.formdata.1:1:1.value[/path],
Re: [path]application.opportunity.id[/path]
[path]application.opportunity.title_labels.label_text[/path]


Congratulations on your provisional job offer.

Steps to take immediately

You now need to complete a Disclosure Scotland (DS) application:

https://www.mygov.scot/basic-disclosure/apply-for-basic-disclosure

We encourage you to apply online using the “Apply Now” functionality, which is significantly quicker.

When you open the application you will be asked ‘Are you applying for a disclosure for a coronavirus role in Scotland?’ Select No to this question.

If you are not able to apply online, a paper application is route available:

https://www.mygov.scot/disclosure-paper-application

When your criminal record check is complete

A disclosure certificate will be sent directly to your home address. To complete your pre-employment checks, we will need sight of the original certificate.

To prevent delay please send your certificate with a covering letter asking for the certificate to be returned to you as soon as possible to:
