Job status since 6th April


This information is essential to ensure that your tax information is shared correctly with HMRC.

Select one of the three statements.

Statement A

For most staff transfers statements B or C would apply. For example, a school leaver who hasn't ever had any part-time work and is now starting their first job. They would choose statement A.

Statement B

Choose statement B if you have lost your P45 or did not get one from your last employer or Jobcentre Plus. Remember, if you receive payments from any pensions do not choose statements A or B.

Statement C

Choose statement C if you are in receipt of any pension - state, works or private and/or you are receiving payments from another job.
