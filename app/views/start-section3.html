This section is for line managers of employees transferring across the Civil Service. These include arm’s length bodies (ALBs) and non-departmental public bodies (NDPBs).

The process is also known as:

  • other government departments (OGD) transfer process
  • staff transfers within the Civil Service.

In this section

You will review the employee’s answers to sections 1 and 2.

To complete this section you also need some information about the employee:

  • recruitment through fair and open competition
  • loan end date (if they are on loan to another department)
  • probationary period (if they are on a probationary period)
  • disciplinary record (if any)
  • annual leave arrangements
  • date of transfer

You can complete Section 3 all at once or return later.
